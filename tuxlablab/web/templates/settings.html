Lab Settings

Settings are stored in the SQLite database. Changes take effect immediately for new operations.

Format notes: host accepts an IP, hostname, or localhost; port must be 1-65535; labgw, labdhcpstart, and labdhcpend must be valid IPv4 addresses, and DHCP start must be less than or equal to DHCP end.

Red Hat downloads: RHEL images are downloaded directly from the Red Hat CDN using your system's entitlement certificates (installed by subscription-manager). Certificates are auto-discovered from /etc/pki/entitlement/ and /etc/rhsm/ca/redhat-uep.pem. If your certificates are in a non-standard location, set rhn_ca_cert, rhn_entitlement_cert, and rhn_entitlement_key in the table below. rhnusername and rhnpassword are used only for RHSM subscription registration (not for image downloads).
